title: "WBD Consent Solicitations Close With Requisite Consents Received Across All Indentures"
fact: "On May 27, 2026, Paramount Skydance announced the results of the consent solicitations conducted by the two WBD subsidiary issuers, Discovery Global Holdings, Inc. and Discovery Communications, LLC. The issuers received the requisite consents, and the solicitations expired at 5:00 p.m. New York time on May 26, 2026. Supplemental indentures to all three existing WBD indentures were executed on May 26, 2026. The supplemental indentures became effective on execution but only become operative on the consent payment date, expected on or about May 29, 2026. Paramount funded the consent payment using cash on hand, on the WBD issuers' behalf, and is obligated to fund it whether or not the acquisition closes. The press release confirmed that the tender and exchange offers expire June 17, 2026, subject to extension, with settlement conditioned on the closing of the acquisition. Paramount stated it currently anticipates extending the offer expiration date until the time of consummation of the acquisition."
context: "The consent leg cleared without drama, which is the outcome the timeline needed. Receiving requisite consents on all three indentures removes the gating condition that sat in front of Paramount's tender and exchange offers. The detail that matters for processing teams is in the last line of the release: Paramount intends to extend the June 17 expiration to track the closing rather than let the offers lapse and relaunch. That means holders who have tendered should expect the offer to roll forward to the acquisition close, with withdrawal rights preserved until the expiration as extended."
