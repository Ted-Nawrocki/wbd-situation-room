title: "WBD Shareholders Overwhelmingly Approve Paramount Skydance Merger"
fact: "On April 23, 2026, Warner Bros. Discovery stockholders voted at a Special Meeting to approve the transaction with Paramount Skydance Corporation. Preliminary results showed over 1.7 billion votes in favor against approximately 16.3 million against. Shareholders did not approve the executive compensation package presented at the same meeting. The transaction remains subject to regulatory clearances from the U.S. Department of Justice Antitrust Division and European regulators. Both companies continue to target closing in Q3 2026. Paramount confirmed it had successfully completed equity and debt syndications and reported progress across regulatory approvals."
context: "A vote of 1.7 billion to 16.3 million removes shareholder opposition as a risk factor. With equity financing and debt syndications complete, the remaining gating items are regulatory. The risk profile changed materially from three months ago. The failed say-on-pay vote is a footnote. Advisory compensation votes in deal contexts rarely reflect views on the transaction itself, and no institutional investor voted against the merger over the pay package."
