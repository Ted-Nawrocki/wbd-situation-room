### WBD Consent Solicitations (May 2026)

**Form 8-K: Commencement of Consent Solicitations** (May 19, 2026)  
WBD announcement that subsidiaries Discovery Global Holdings, Inc. and Discovery Communications, LLC have launched consent solicitations across 17 series of outstanding notes; consent deadline May 26, 2026  
[WBD Investor Relations Press Release](https://ir.wbd.com/news-and-events/financial-news/financial-news-details/2026/Warner-Bros--Discovery-Announces-Commencement-of-Consent-Solicitations/default.aspx)

Full terms, conditions, and note series detail for the consent solicitations; issuers are Discovery Global Holdings, Inc. (DGH) and Discovery Communications, LLC (DCL)  
Available via Global Bondholder Services Corporation (tabulation and information agent) and solicitation agents BofA Securities and Citigroup

**Form 8-K: Consent Solicitation Results and Supplemental Indentures** (Filed May 2026)  
WBD received the requisite consents across all three existing WBD indentures. Supplemental indentures effectuating the proposed amendments were executed May 26, 2026, effective at execution and operative upon the consent payment date (on or about May 29, 2026). Amendments cover approximately $17.2 billion in senior unsecured notes. The press release is attached as Exhibit 99.1.

Paramount announcement confirming receipt of requisite consents and execution of supplemental indentures; restates that the tender and exchange offers expire June 17, 2026, subject to extension, with settlement conditioned on the closing of the acquisition, and that Paramount anticipates extending the expiration to the time of consummation

### Acquisition Financing (May - June 2026)

**Bridge Loan Replacement Financing -- Expanded Term Loan**  
WBD's term loan to replace short-term bridge financing was increased to a total of $15 billion, up from approximately $10 billion, by a JPMorgan-led bank group, reportedly on strong demand, ahead of the acquisition. Attributed to financial press reporting; the corresponding credit documentation should be confirmed via WBD's and Paramount's subsequent SEC filings.  
[Investing.com - 'Paramount receives consents for WBD debt amendments,' May 2026](https://www.investing.com/news/company-news/paramount-receives-consents-for-wbd-debt-amendments-93CH-4712420)
