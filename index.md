## The situation room

A corporate actions drama is unfolding around **Warner Bros. Discovery**.

It has all the elements practitioners recognize immediately. A leveraged company born from a complex separation. A balance sheet that dictates strategy. A carefully staged restructuring. And now, competing suitors with very different endgames, forcing shareholders, boards, and operations teams into a compressed decision window.

This site exists to track that drama as it plays out, using public filings, reputable reporting, and the perspective of people who understand what actually happens once the press release is over.
